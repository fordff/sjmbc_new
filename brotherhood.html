Our Men's Ministry

Ministry Info


Our Men’s Ministry is a place for the men of the church to find fellowship, accountability and encouragement. Men will find meaningful and edifying experiences by participating in small groups, events and activities offered. All men are encouraged to take advantage of these opportunities to grow in their relationship with God and other brothers. We meet Every month on tuesday , we gather together to eat, fellowship, and listen to a talk about relative topics which are the core of men’s lives.

Service is the overflow of discipleship. It seeks to extend God's grace and mercy to others for His glory and not our own. We Serve as an expression of love and stewarrdship of grace, marked by humility, generosity and hospitality

Do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that by testing you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect.
Romans 12:2

The mission of the Men's ministry is to inspire and disciple men to be spirtural leaders in their homes, places of business and church.

    When a man follows the principles of biblical manhood, those around him benefit from his leadership and care. Kingdom Man challenges and equips men to fully understand their position under God as well as their position over what God has given them. The biblical definition of a man is one who has learned to operate under the authority of Jesus Christ while carrying out responsible and legitimate leadership within the sphere of influence that God has placed him. Kingdom Man provides concepts men can follow that will help them to actively pursue ways to maximize and develop the character qualities of biblical manhood in their lives.
