Music Ministry

Ministry Info


The Music Ministry of our church has a variety of opportunities for people of all ages to serve Christ through music. Whether you’ve been performing for a lifetime or you want to begin to “make a joyful noise unto the Lord”, there’s a place for you in our Music Ministry.

If you are a member of St. John Missionary Baptist Church, gifted musically ,and more importantly, if you have a desire to use your gifts for God's Glory, then we would love to have you join our music team

"...be filled with the Spirit, addressing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and playing to the Lord in your heart" Ephesians 5: 18-19
