Our Youth

Ministry Info

Young Adults are the futrue of the church. This group is made up of young adults and seeks to do effective outreach and community building for young adults age 17-35.

Life can get complicated once you cross that nebulous threshold into adulthood. There are complicated decisions. Bigger stakes. Serious relationships. And a whole lot more decisions. The young adult group of our church provides a welcoming atmosphere for college and early-career aged young people

Like newborn infants, long for the pure spiritual milk, that by it you may grow up into salvation.
1 Peter 2:2

Ministry in Action

A young branch takes on all the bends that one gives to it.

We invite you to join us at any or all of our regularly scheduled worship services, prayer meetings or Bible studies. We look forward to fellowshipping with you!
