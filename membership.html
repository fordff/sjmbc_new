Come be apart of St. John

We’re excited to have you consider St. John as the place you want to belong.

We welcome visitors who enjoy learning about God’s Word ,but pray that you’ll choose to become a full member of Christ’s family, by actively participating in the life of St. John Missionary Baptist Church. The first step in this process is to attend an upcoming New Members Class.
This class is both informative and fun and allows you the opportunity to meet a few of the church staff and elders as well as ask questions. This class is your portal to serving at St. John MBC and we’d be honored to have you come learn what St. John offers your family.

The best way to teach character is to have it around the house.
