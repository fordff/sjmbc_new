Women's Ministry

Ministry Info


We endeavor to help women build a firm foundation for their lives through the study of God’s Word and experiencing the power of the Holy Spirit. Since women truly are the heart of the home, they set the climate and atmosphere in which the household functions. So the attitude of the heart needs to be nurtured and trained in hearing God’s voice and thereby exemplifying His love and care.

Living & Sharing in God's Amazing Grace. The women's ministry is open to women of all ages , however there are smaller groups that make up the women's ministry umbrella. Women’s groups give the ladies in our church family special times of interaction. Our women’s groups, located in several locations throughout our community, are times of Bible study, prayer (and, of course, food). These groups provide a refreshing discipleship opportunity for the women in the church.
