About Us

St. John Missionary Baptist Church is an exciting, life-giving church with a heart for the Trinity. Under the leadership of Dr. Antionio M. Alfred, St. John Missionary Baptist Church is committed to sharing the hope of Jesus through Bible-centered teaching and community.

People of all ages, races, and backgrounds are welcome on Sunday mornings and throughout the week to pursue God through worship gatherings, small groups, kids and student ministries, and various outreach projects.
