OUR MISSION

We serve a Winner in Christ Jesus by living the Word in our daily lives. As a local body of believers in Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior, we are called to live the Word victoriously in the world as we give witness to the love and power of God.

Our Strategy

To equip our members to lead with integrity, be faithful servants and steward, and make disciples for the Lord through Christ-centered counseling.

Our Vision

Serving a winner by living the word - Romans 6:1-14

Our Values

Integrity, Service, Stewardship, Discipleship - Matthew 22:36-40
